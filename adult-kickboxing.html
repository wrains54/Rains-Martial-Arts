About This Program

Train Like You Mean It.

Adult Kickboxing is the most intense group class at RMA. Combining technical kickboxing instruction with conditioning, pad work, and partner drills — this class builds real skill while delivering an incredible workout. No experience needed.

What You'll Learn
Punches, kicks, elbows, and combinations
Heavy bag and focus mitt work
Partner drills and sparring fundamentals
Movement, footwork, and ring strategy
Cardio conditioning and athleticism
Who It's For

Adults 15 and up of all backgrounds and fitness levels. Whether you've never thrown a punch or have years of training — this class is built to challenge and develop you. Coming from someone with zero kickboxing experience, Will makes classes beginner friendly while also making it intense and entertaining.
