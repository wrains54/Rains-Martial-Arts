Welcome, Shoppers

What to Expect as a Buyer

Cottagelady sales are known for fair prices, quality items, and a welcoming atmosphere. Here’s everything you need to know before you arrive.

💰

Fair, Researched Pricing

Every item is individually priced based on current market values. You’ll find honest prices that reflect what things are actually worth — not inflated or guessed at.

📅

Typical Sale Days

Most Cottagelady sales run Thursday–Saturday or Friday–Sunday, typically 8 AM–4 PM. Exact dates and hours are always listed in the sale announcement.

💳

Payment Methods

We accept cash and all major credit and debit cards. There is no ATM on site, so come prepared if you prefer cash. All sales are final.

🚫

No Early Bird Entry

For fairness to all buyers, no one is admitted before the official opening time. A numbered entry system may be used at high-demand sales — arrive early to secure your number.

🆕

Numbered Entry System

At popular sales, numbered entry cards are distributed before opening. Numbers are called in sequence at open time, giving everyone an equal and orderly opportunity to shop.

🔔

Stay Notified

New sales are announced with 1–2 weeks notice. Contact Donna to be added to her personal notification list and never miss a sale in your area.
