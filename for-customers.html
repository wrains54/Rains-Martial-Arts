What to Expect

A Great Sale Experience

Our sales are well-organized, professionally staffed, and stocked with a diverse mix of items at fair prices. Here is what you need to know before you come.

🏷️
Fair Pricing

Every item is priced based on current market research and Donna's deep expertise. You will find genuine value without inflated estate sale markups.

🗓️
Typical Sale Days

Most sales run Friday, Saturday, and Sunday. Hours and addresses are posted on our Upcoming Sales page and advertised in advance.

💳
Payment Accepted

We accept cash and major credit cards at most sales. Specific payment information is listed in each sale's announcement.

No Early Birds

Please respect the posted start time. Early arrivals cannot be accommodated and may be turned away. We open promptly and welcome all shoppers equally.

For popular sales, we use a numbered sign-in system to manage entry and ensure a fair, orderly experience for all shoppers.
