About This Class

Hit Hard. Move Fast. Train Smart.

Kids Kickboxing is a high-energy, technique-driven program for kids ages 7 and up who want to do more than just go through the motions. Students train real kickboxing fundamentals: punches, kicks, footwork, and combinations, using pads, mitts, and partner drills that make every session fun and physically demanding.

This class is perfect for kids who are naturally athletic and want a challenge, as well as those who need a structured, confidence-building environment to come out of their shell. Our coaches set the tone: high expectations, strong technique, and a lot of encouragement along the way.

Beyond the physical skills, kids learn to control their body under pressure, communicate with training partners, and push through fatigue. These lessons show up in school, sports, and everyday life.

  • Kickboxing strikes, kicks, and combinations with proper technique
  • Pad work and partner drills that build timing and confidence
  • Cardiovascular conditioning and athletic development
  • Focus, emotional regulation, and composure under pressure
  • A fun, safe, and encouraging training environment
