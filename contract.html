Working Together

Contract & Agreement

Our client agreement is straightforward and built on trust. Here is an overview of how we work together.

Commission Structure
Cottagelady Estate Moving & Sales operates on a commission-only basis. There are no upfront fees or charges. Our commission is calculated as a percentage of the total gross sales and is deducted before your proceeds are disbursed. The specific rate is discussed and agreed upon during your initial consultation.
Minimum Requirements
We require a minimum of $5,000 in estimated sellable items to conduct a sale. This ensures the sale is worthwhile for both you and our team. If you are unsure whether your situation meets this threshold, please contact us — we are happy to assess during a free consultation.
Access to the Property
The client agrees to provide Cottagelady Estate Moving & Sales reasonable access to the property during the preparation period and sale days. We request that family members and non-team individuals are not present during the sale to ensure a smooth, professional experience for buyers.
Item Removal Before the Sale
All items to be removed from the sale by family must be identified and removed before preparation begins. Once items have been priced and staged, removing them from the sale may affect the agreed-upon commission structure.
Advertising & Marketing Rights
The client authorizes Cottagelady Estate Moving & Sales to photograph, advertise, and promote the sale and its contents across appropriate platforms, including social media, estate sale listing sites, and email distribution lists.
Payment to Client
Proceeds are distributed to the client following the close of the sale and reconciliation of all transactions, typically within a few business days. A complete accounting of items sold and amounts received is provided with payment.
Remaining Items
Items not sold during the sale may be donated to local non-profits of your choosing, with Cottagelady facilitating the donation and providing tax receipt documentation. Arrangements for remaining items are discussed and agreed upon before the sale begins.

Ready to Review the Full Agreement?

The complete contract is reviewed and signed during your initial consultation. Contact Donna to schedule your free, no-obligation meeting.
