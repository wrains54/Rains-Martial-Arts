Kids Kickboxing & Muay Thai

Authentic Striking Training for Kids

At Rains Martial Arts, our Kids Kickboxing & Muay Thai program introduces young students to dynamic striking skills rooted in authentic Muay Thai and modern kickboxing. Led by Head Instructor Will Rains, IKF World Champion, this program blends technique, conditioning, and discipline to create a safe and inspiring training environment.

Students build confidence, respect, and resilience while learning punches, kicks, knees, and elbows. Parents often notice improved focus, stronger grades, and healthier habits — and many choose to train alongside their children, creating a shared journey of growth.

Program Highlights

  • Fundamentals of kickboxing and Muay Thai striking
  • Pad work and partner drills for skill development
  • Conditioning for speed, strength, and endurance
  • Safe, supervised sparring opportunities
  • Discipline and respect emphasized every class
