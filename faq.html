FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before walking through the door.

What age can my child start martial arts?

Our Young Warriors program accepts children starting at age 4. Kids ages 7 and up can join Kids Karate or Kids Kickboxing. We recommend starting young — the earlier kids build focus and discipline, the better the long-term results.

Do I need any prior experience?

No experience needed — all classes are designed to be beginner-friendly. Our instructors start every student from the ground up and progress at a pace that works for them.

How does the free trial work?

Click "Start Free Trial" on any page, fill out the short form, and we'll contact you to confirm your class time. You'll come in, try the class at no cost, and decide if it's the right fit — zero pressure.

What should my child wear to their first class?

Comfortable athletic clothes — shorts and a t-shirt work perfectly. No shoes needed on the mat. You do not need to buy a uniform or any gear before your first class.

How much does a membership cost?

Individual memberships start at $100/month. The Double Trouble partner plan is $150/month for two students. The Family Plan covers up to 4 family members for $250/month. All plans include up to 4 group classes per week. See full pricing →

What is the difference between Kids Karate and Kids Kickboxing?

Kids Karate focuses on traditional karate structure, forms, stances, and controlled striking — Mon–Thu at 4:30 PM. Kids Kickboxing is a higher-energy class focused on combinations, pad work, and conditioning — Tue & Thu at 5:30 PM. Many families pick based on their child's personality and schedule.

Can parents watch their child's class?

Yes — parents are always welcome to watch. We believe in keeping families involved and informed throughout their child's martial arts journey.

Is there a contract or cancellation fee?

Memberships require a 3-month commitment. After that, you can cancel anytime with no additional fees. We earn your continued membership every month by delivering great training.
