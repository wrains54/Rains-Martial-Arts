Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Cottagelady Estate Moving & Sales.

How much will my estate sale bring in?
Upon our initial consultation, we provide a baseline estimate of what your sale could realistically generate. This may change if additional items are discovered during preparation or if items are removed before the sale. We are always honest about our projections.
What percentage of items typically sell?
We sell 90–95% of items in 99% of our sales. Our experienced pricing, staging, and marketing means very little is left unsold at the end of your sale.
How long does it take to prepare for a sale?
Most sales require 1 to 2 weeks of preparation — sometimes only one week is needed. We use that time to organize, clean, research, price, and stage every item, as well as launch our marketing campaign to bring in buyers.
How long does the actual sale run?
A typical estate sale is a 2 to 3 day event, usually held Friday, Saturday, and Sunday. Our team is on-site throughout to assist buyers and manage the sale professionally.
Do I have to pay anything upfront?
No — never. We operate on a commission basis only. You pay nothing until after the sale is complete and items have sold. Our success is directly tied to yours.
Is there a minimum requirement?
Yes — we ask that there be a minimum of $5,000 worth of items to be sold. If you are unsure whether your situation qualifies, simply contact us for a free consultation and we will assess together.
What happens if items are found after family has cleaned out the house?
This is very common — even after families have gone through a home thoroughly, additional items are frequently discovered during our preparation process. We box those items up and arrange for pickup or, if family is out of the area, coordinate shipping.
What happens to items that don't sell?
We can provide a list of local non-profit organizations that accept donations, or we can arrange donations directly and provide you with a tax receipt. Our goal is to leave your home completely clear after the sale.
How do you price items?
Donna has spent years developing expertise in antiques, furniture, collectibles, and everyday household items. For unfamiliar pieces, she researches current market values to ensure everything is priced fairly for both sellers and buyers.
How soon can we get started?
Reach out and we'll schedule a free consultation at your earliest convenience. From there, we move at a pace that works for your situation — whether you need us right away or prefer to plan ahead.

Still have questions? We're happy to help.
