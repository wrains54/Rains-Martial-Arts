About Donna

Experienced. Trusted. Local.

Donna Long has called Cookeville home for decades. With over 10 years of estate sale experience, 20 years at Tennessee Tech University, and a deep background in local real estate, she has built a devoted network of buyers and sellers throughout the Upper Cumberland region.

As a proud member of the American Society of Estate Liquidators, Donna brings professional standards and genuine compassion to every sale — specializing in helping old treasures find new owners who will love them just as much.
