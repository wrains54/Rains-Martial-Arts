Hi, I’m Donna — and I genuinely care about your family

For over a decade, I’ve helped Middle Tennessee families navigate some of life’s most significant transitions — downsizing, moving a parent into care, or lovingly clearing a family home after a loss.

Every piece of furniture, every box of memories, every heirloom has a story. I treat your belongings with the same care I’d want for my own family’s things. My job is to make this process feel manageable, dignified, and — when possible — even meaningful.

As a commission-only specialist, my success depends entirely on yours. There are never any upfront fees, hidden charges, or surprises. Just honest, hardworking service you can count on.

How It Works
✧

Four Simple Steps to a Successful Sale

We’ve guided dozens of Tennessee families through this process. Here’s what to expect.

1

Free Consultation

We visit your home, walk through the contents, and tell you honestly whether an estate sale makes sense — no cost, no pressure.

2

Staging & Pricing

Over 1–2 weeks we organize, stage, research, and individually price every item to attract buyers and maximize your return.

3

The Sale

We run a 2–3 day sale with professional staffing, payment processing, and organized crowd management. You don’t need to be there.

4

You Get Paid

Within days of the sale’s close you receive a full itemized accounting and your payment — clear, honest, and on time.
