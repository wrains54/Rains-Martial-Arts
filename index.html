About Us

Rains Martial Arts

At Rains Martial Arts, every class is built on a foundation of discipline, respect, and authentic training. Our school was founded by Will Rains, a martial artist whose journey has taken him from earning a 2nd Degree Black Belt in Taekwondo, to becoming an IKF World Champion Kickboxer, to training Muay Thai in Thailand

If you want someone who will take your training seriously, create opportunities, and fight alongside you, Rains Martial Arts is where you want to be. Martial Arts is not just about learning to fight, its about the discipline you learn from training that carries on throughout every aspect of life.

Benefits

Why Train Martial Arts?

Confidence & Discipline

Build self‑confidence and discipline through structured training that challenges you to grow.

Strength & Fitness

Improve endurance, flexibility, and overall health with dynamic workouts that engage the whole body.

Better Grades

Students often see improved academic performance as martial arts training builds responsibility, time management, and study habits.

Focus & Mental Clarity

Sharpen concentration, reduce stress, and carry focus into school, work, and everyday life.
