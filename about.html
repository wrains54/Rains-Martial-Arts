About Us

Rains Martial Arts

Rains Martial Arts was built on the idea that martial arts should be taught with structure, discipline, and authenticity — not watered down fitness classes or belt factories.

Founded by Will Rains, the school reflects a lifelong journey through traditional martial arts and modern combat sports. From earning a 2nd Degree Black Belt in Taekwondo, to becoming an IKF World Champion Kickboxer, to training and fighting in Muay Thai gyms in Thailand, every experience has shaped how we train.

That background means students learn more than just techniques — they learn how to move with purpose, think under pressure, and build confidence that carries into school, work, and everyday life.

Whether you’re a beginner, a parent looking for the right environment for your child, or an experienced martial artist looking for real training, Rains Martial Arts is a place where progress is earned and respected.
