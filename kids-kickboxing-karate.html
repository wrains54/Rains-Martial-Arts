About This Class

Real Technique. Real Discipline. Real Growth.

Our Kids Karate program is built for children ages 7 and up who are ready to train seriously. Based in Taekwondo fundamentals, the curriculum gives students a structured path from beginner to advanced, with clear belt milestones along the way to keep motivation high and progress visible.

Every class balances technical instruction with conditioning, partner drills, and controlled sparring work. Students learn to strike with power and precision, move with purpose, and carry the values of respect and discipline into every area of their life.

With four class times per week, it's easy to find a slot that works for your family's schedule. Many of our students train multiple days a week and see faster progress as a result. Whether your child is a complete beginner or has some martial arts background, they'll be welcomed and challenged appropriately.

  • Traditional karate stances, forms, and striking technique
  • Structured belt rank progression with clear goals
  • Controlled partner work and pad drills to build timing
  • Physical conditioning: strength, agility, and endurance
  • Discipline, focus, and respect on and off the mat
