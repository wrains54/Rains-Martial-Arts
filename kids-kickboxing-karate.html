About This Program

Karate That Kids Take Seriously

Kids Karate teaches the real techniques of traditional karate. Students move up through belt ranks as their skill grows, and the discipline they build shows up outside the gym too.

What They'll Learn
Karate strikes, blocks, and kicks
Forms (kata) and combinations
Discipline and focus training
Respect, self-control, and mat etiquette
Belt progression and goal-setting
Who It's For

For kids ages 6 to 12 of all skill levels. Kids who have never trained before and kids coming from other programs both fit right in. Your first 3 classes are free.
