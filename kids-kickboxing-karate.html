Youth Kickboxing Karate

A high-energy youth program that blends karate structure with practical kickboxing fundamentals. Kids build discipline, confidence, coordination, and real striking mechanics in a supportive environment.

This class is a strong fit for students who need focus, structure, and a place to grow mentally and physically. Parents are welcome to be part of the journey.

What They’ll Work On

  • Karate stance work, forms, and fundamentals
  • Kickboxing drills, pad work, and movement
  • Conditioning, coordination, and body control
  • Discipline, respect, and perseverance
  • Clear progression and real skill development
